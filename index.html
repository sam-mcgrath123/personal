Projects

WeightWorks

Computer Science 309 is a course at Iowa State where students are tasked with creating an app over the course of a semester. I started this project after I finished that class because, with deadlines to meet for the class, it wasn't possible to really understand the best practices for mobile app development and get up to speed with newer technologies that have made android development more efficient. By using the app my team and I made during our fall semester of 2022 as a model, I'm attempting to completely rework the design of both the front end and backend to eventually build a fitness app that could be realistically used.
